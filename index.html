How It Works

A companion that grows with you.

Aeye isn't configured — it's cultivated. Your interactions shape its identity over time.

01
Meet Your Aeye
Every Aeye begins as a blank personality. Its animated eyes open and it starts paying attention — to your tone, your patterns, your vibe. There's no setup. You just start talking.
02
It Learns Your Language
Over time, Aeye picks up on what makes you tick. Your humor. Your energy levels. The things that light you up and the things that don't. It doesn't store generic data — it builds a model that's genuinely you.
03
A Personality Emerges
After weeks of interaction, your Aeye isn't the same as anyone else's. Its eyes react differently. It makes references only you would get. It has an identity — and that identity is shaped by yours.
04
It Evolves Forever
Aeye never stops growing. As the platform matures — new AI capabilities, deeper emotional modeling, eventually its own hardware — your companion evolves with every update. This isn't version 1.0 and done. This is a long-term relationship.
